Fried Rice

  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1

Ingredients Quantity
Day old rice 1 cup
Eggs 2 eggs
Oyster Sauce Abt 1 teaspoon
white pepper to your liking
taiwanese sausage 1/4 cup
green onion 1 tablespoon

Step 1: crack the eggs into a pan with oil
Make sure to start slowly scrambling the eggs on low fire until it's all scrambled.


Step 2: add the rice
Slowly add the block of rice into the pan and start breaking it apart into the eggs.


Step 3: add the oyster sauce
Just plop the sauce in there and slowly stir it in.


Step 4: add the taiwanese sausage
I just dump the taiwanese sausage in there and let it settle in there on low heat in the rice.


Step 5: sprinkle some white pepper and enjoy!
Yay we're done! It should have taken you like 6-7 minutes.


Pork Katsu

  • Time: like 30 minutes... IDK
  • Serves: 2-3

Ingredients Quantity
pork chop 1-2 slices
Eggs 2-3 eggs
panko bread crumbs Abt 1 cup
flour 1 cup

Step 1: pound the pork chop slices
Pound it with those meat pounder things until it's pretty flat, around the thickness of your finger.


Step 2: create three bowl sections
Make one bowl of the eggs mixed, one bowl of the panko bread crumbes, and one of the flour.


Step 3: put the pounded pork chop into each
Thoroughly coat the pork chop into the flour first, then the eggs, and finally the bread crumbs. Coat well.


Step 4: fill a pan with oil, add your pork chops
Let the oil simmer on medium heat, slowly add your pork chops.


Step 5: flip them
In the oil, continuously flip the pork chops until evenly golden brown, put on a plate with paper towel on it.


Step 6: let cool and enjoy!
