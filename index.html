Fried Rice

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Day old rice 1 cup
Eggs 2 eggs
Oyster Sauce Abt 1 teaspoon
white pepper to your liking
taiwanese sausage 1/4 cup
green onion 1 tablespoon

Step 1: crack the eggs into a pan with oil
Make sure to start slowly scrambling the eggs on low fire until it's all scrambled.
Step 2: add the rice
Slowly add the block of rice into the pan and start breaking it apart into the eggs.
Step 3: add the oyster sauce Just plop the sauce in there and slowly stir it in.
Step 4: add the taiwanese sausage I just dump the taiwanese sausage in there and let it settle in there on low heat in the rice.
Stpe 5: sprinkle some white pepper and enjoy! Yay we're done! It should have taken you like 6-7 minutes.
